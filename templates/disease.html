About project

We have build a system in which our system will predict diseases by X-rays and MRI or Symptoms of the user. Users may or can be suffering from diseases like pneumonia , Covid , Brain tumor and breast cancer . So simply a user has to upload X-rays and MRI Image even put the symptoms according to which he or she is suffering from after uploading, our system will predict on the basis of deep learning algorithms , whether that person is suffering from any particular disease for which he or she has uploaded the image or symptoms.

After getting the result if person is suffering from any disease then we will provide an information related to that disease and if possible we will provide natural remedies and first Aid , If that person is not suffering from any diseases then we’ll provide the information to stay fit so that person should have less chance to suffering from that disease in future.
